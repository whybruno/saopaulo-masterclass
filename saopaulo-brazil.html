Digital Transformation Masterclass

About:

Global Policy House, in collaboration with its partners, is organizing a Digital Transformation Masterclass from 18th to 21st October in Sao Paolo, Brazil.

This masterclass is designed to provide an in-depth exploration of Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain Technology to support trade and economic growth. Participants will gain a comprehensive understanding of how these cutting-edge technologies can revolutionize businesses and industries. From machine learning to smart contracts, attendees will learn about the latest trends and applications shaping the way we work and live.

Expert instructors will lead hands-on exercises and present case studies, ensuring participants leave the masterclass empowered and ready to implement their new knowledge in their careers. This event is ideal for anyone looking to stay ahead of the curve in today’s digital world.

The Digital Transformation Masterclass aims to attract a unique audience of international development practitioners, government officials, thought leaders, investors, financiers, and development partners. It provides an excellent opportunity for organizations to introduce their programs and services, build lasting relationships, and collaborate well beyond the event.
